Amala and Ewedu

My Plate of Amala

Amala and Ewedu is an indigenous meal of the yoruba speaking Nigerians. It is a delicacy made for yam flour

Ingredients for make Amala

  • yam flour
  • cassava flour
  • water

Below is a step by step procedure for making amala

  1. boil water
  2. stair amala in water
  3. dish it in plates
